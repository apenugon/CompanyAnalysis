Amper Music

Company Analysis

Who?

I decided to make this project about a company I found out about recently: Amper Music. They just raised a $4M Seed round in March, and I met a founder earlier this year. But what interests me about them is they have created a platform to create AI-generated music! As an avid musician myself (and one-time aspiring producer myself) I was hooked.

See the Github for more information! Also, this page is loaded live every time, so you may need to wait for the graphs to load.

What?

I was curious if I could use information online to determine something about the finances of this company. There were two sources of information that were great: WebArchive, which I used to look at the growth of their customer showcase over time to forecast revenue, and Amazon Alexa data, which I used to estimate platform usage (and therefore costs). The graph on the left depicts Amper's estimated costs and revenue to date, month over month.

To estimate revenue, I estimated average deal size as about $8000, from here. I also estimated that every customer in the showcase counted as a deal.

The model I created relies on data from BuiltWith for info about their tech stack. I estimated page visits from Alexa ranking using this information. I assumed that 25% of those visits could translate to an hour of platform usage, costing $0.10 on Amazon. I also used their address on their WHOIS to get real estate cost. I modelled employee cost assuming that they scaled linearly with revenue, and that the cost for each was $130k on average.
